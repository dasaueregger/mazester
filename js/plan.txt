    one Boardopbject
    a list of Ballobjects
    a list of Playerobjects
    a factorymethod for Ballobjects
    a factorymethod for Playerobjects
    a ballsmovemethod for Ballobjects

    a Grid

    a Setmazemethod

    coordinates
    activestate

    a getstate
    a setstate
    a getcoordinates

    a Ballpaintmethod
    a Ballmoveanimationmethod
